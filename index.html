Persian Radio, Anywhere

Stream the best Persian radio stations live on your iPhone — free, simple, and beautiful.

Features

Everything you need in a radio app.

📻

Live Persian Stations

Stream dozens of Persian radio stations with a single tap. Curated station list updated automatically, so you always have the best selection.

⏱️

Sleep Timer

Set a sleep timer and drift off to your favorite station. The radio stops automatically so you don't have to worry about it.

⏺️

Record & Save

Record live audio directly to your device as MP3 files. Capture songs, shows, or moments you want to keep forever.

📅

Persian Calendar

Stay connected to the Persian calendar right inside the app. Beautiful SwiftUI calendar view with full date navigation.

🚶

Step Tracking

Track your steps and floors climbed — today and yesterday — while listening. A built-in pedometer keeps you moving.

🎵

Now Playing & YouTube

See what's playing with rich Lock Screen and Control Center integration. Tap to find the current track on YouTube right inside the app.

Privacy

Your data stays on your device.

🔒

Local Processing

All audio streaming, recording, and step tracking data is processed entirely on your device. Nothing is uploaded to external servers.

🛡️

No Data Sharing

We never collect, store, or share your personal information with third parties. Your listening habits and recordings are yours alone.

📋

Transparent Ads

The app displays Google AdMob ads. You can manage your ad privacy preferences at any time in Settings, including opting out of personalized ads.
