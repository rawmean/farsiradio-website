Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

Our Commitment to Privacy

FarsiRadio is designed with your privacy in mind. We believe you should be able to enjoy Persian radio without worrying about who has access to your data.

Data Collection

We do not collect any user data or information.

  • No personal information is collected by the app.
  • No usage data or analytics are gathered.
  • No user information is stored on any servers.
  • No user information is shared with third parties.

All audio streaming, recording, and step tracking data is processed entirely on your device. Your listening habits, recordings, and activity data never leave your iPhone.

Third-Party Services

YouTube API Services

FarsiRadio uses YouTube API Services to provide in-app YouTube video lookup for currently playing tracks. By using this feature, you are also bound by YouTube's terms:

  • YouTube Terms of Service
  • Google Privacy Policy

Google AdMob

The app displays advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may use device identifiers for personalized advertising, subject to your consent choices. You can manage your ad privacy preferences at any time in the app's Settings, including opting out of personalized ads via the Google User Messaging Platform consent dialog.

Children's Privacy

FarsiRadio does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at apps@maadotaa.com.
